Biography

Jiangjie Chen (ÈôàÊ±üÊç∑) is a third-year Ph.D. candidate at Fudan University (FDU) in School of Computer Science, Shanghai, China, where he is advised by Prof. Yanghua Xiao at Knowledge Works Lab. He is also currently a research intern at ByteDance AI Lab, where he works closely with Prof. Lei Li (now at UCSB), Dr. Hao Zhou and Dr. Changzhi Sun.

He is a zealous believer in reasoning over natural language and dedicated to make neural models being right for the right reasons. His research interests mainly lie in Explainable NLP, Text Generation and Commonsense Reasoning, and particularly the deep integration of them. His previous research interests also include Knowledge Graph Construction (KGC).
